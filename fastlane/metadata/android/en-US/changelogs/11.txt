*Beta release 1*

* After a lots of preview release Last Launcher entered into beta phase.

* What's New in this release

- Search through Apps.
- Performance improvements(startup, sorting and Random color).
- Big reduction(23%) in apk size.
- Further memory consumption reduction.

- This update will remove old shortcut from this launcher so you readded them after this update.
